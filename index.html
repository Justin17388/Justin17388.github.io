A little bit about us:
My name is Justin and I am married to a therapist. I got into 3D printing about a year ago. After printing several Star Wars ships and synth parts,
I started test making small toys and props for our kids and friends. Our youngest daughter has ADHD and had some issues picking at her nails
so my wife suggested I print her a fidget ring. She loved it and has since stopped picking. I started printing other types of sensory
toys not just for her, but for my wife's practice to give out at conventions. It was at those conventions that I realized how much help
these items could be to therapists, their clients, and other parents. These fidgets help those with anxiety, ADHD, autism, and so much more.
So check us out and see what we can do for you. We have multiple standard products and the ability to make specific custom items if needed.
